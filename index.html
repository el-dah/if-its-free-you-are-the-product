Etiquette Guide / 礼仪指南

Please read all instructions carefully before beginning. / 开始之前，请仔细阅读以下说明。

Instructions / 操作说明

  1. While viewing the instructional film on the adjacent screen, follow the gestures demonstrated.

    请观看旁边屏幕上的教学影片，并跟随示范动作进行练习。

  2. Pay close attention to the manner in which your movements generate speech on screen.

    请仔细观察您的动作如何在屏幕上生成文字。

  3. Hold each pose for a moment and allow the text to emerge.

    请保持每个姿势片刻，让文字逐渐显现。

  4. Observe how your gestures animate a conversation between texts, and consider your place within it, how it applies your own unique context.

    观察你的手势如何使文本之间的对话动画化，并考虑你在其中的位置，它如何应用你自己独特的语境

  5. Consider whether you feel understood, or merely commented upon.

    请思考：您感到被理解了吗？还是仅仅成为了被评论的对象？

  6. As you proceed, reflect upon your right to self-determination in all circumstances.

    在继续操作的过程中，请思考您在任何情况下所拥有的自主决定权。

Notes to the Reader

In your practice as a housewife today, remember that your right to exist beyond this role—whether online, at work, or elsewhere, regardless of gender—can only be the result of movements led by women before you.

在今天扮演家庭主妇这一角色时，请记住：您得以超越这一身份而存在的权利——无论是在网络空间、职场之中，还是其他领域——都来自于前人女性所推动的社会运动与抗争。
