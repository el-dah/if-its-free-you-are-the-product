Welcome Home / 欢迎回家

Make yourself comfortable.

请随意使用这个空间。

You are welcome to rest, tidy the space, move the towels, prepare food, or follow the actions demonstrated on the adjacent screen.

您可以休息、整理空间、移动毛巾与水桶、准备食物，或跟随旁边屏幕中的动作。

If preparing food for others or leaving food behind, please wear the gloves provided.

如果您为他人准备食物或留下食物供他人食用，请佩戴提供的手套。

While You're Here / 当您在这里时

Your movements will be interpreted by a computer vision system and connected to a collection of texts.

您的动作将被计算机视觉系统解读，并与一组文本建立联系。

Consider:
Who is idealized for performing acts of care or cleaning?
Who around you here notices your labor, and is it before or after you've left the space?
What changes when housework is performed for an audience?
Who benefits when everyday life becomes content?

请思考：
期望谁做家务？
你周围的人会注意到你的劳动，是在你离开后才注意到的吗？
当为观众做家务时，会有什么变化？
当日常生活变得满足时，谁会受益？

About the Texts / 关于文本

The words that appear were written by feminist authors questioning why women are so often expected to gain recognition through care, domestic labor, and service to others.

As you read, consider the growing popularity of domestic lifestyle content online and whether these reflections feel distant, contemporary, compelling, confusing, or intimate.

Texts adapted from writings by Silvia Federici and bell hooks.

出现的话是由女权主义作家写的，质疑为什么女性经常通过照顾、家务劳动和为他人服务来获得认可。

当你阅读时，考虑国内生活方式内容在网上越来越受欢迎，以及这些反思是感觉遥远的、现代的、引人注目的、令人困惑的还是亲密的。

改编自Silvia Federici和bell hooks的著作。
